Jurassic Siesta, Ceratosaurus nasicornis by Wayne Barlowe as seen in the book The Alien Life of Wayne Barlowe (1995). (photocopy of the original sketch on the last slide from Wayne Barlowe’s Insta account @waynebarlowe_thedarkness )

“For all of my paleo work i developed my own, time-saving technique for doing scales. I laid down a modelled base of the very matte Lascaux acrylics and the used a super-sharpened Prismacolor pencil to surface map the zillions of round scales. I would then go back in and either highlight or darken the scales as desired. It worked nicely! Hope you all like it!” -WB

Thank you again for allow me over and over to share your amazing work.
