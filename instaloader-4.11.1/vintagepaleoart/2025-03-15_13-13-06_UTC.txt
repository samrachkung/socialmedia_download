Herrerasaurus by Douglas Henderson from the book How Dinosaurs Came to Be by Patricia Lauber (1996). 

“Herrerasaurus belonged to a group that branched off from the first dinosaurs. Ten to 20 feet long, it weighed 300 to 450 pounds. Its jaws and hands show that it was a fearsome animal.”
